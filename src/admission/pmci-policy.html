Policy on Withdrawal of Funds


A student who will withdraw his/her enrollment prior to the start of classes will be charged in full the pertinent registration fee. A student who will drop out or withdraw within one week after the first day of class will be charged 10% of the total tuition fee and the pertinent registration fee. If he/she withdraws within 2 weeks after the start of classes, he/she will be charged 20% of the tuition fee plus the pertinent registration fee. In the event a student withdraws/drops out two weeks after the start of classes, he/she will be charged the entire other chargers, miscellaneous fees and other school fees plus the tuition fee for the month attended. However, if the transfer or withdrawal is due to a justifiable reason, the student shall be charged the pertinent fees only up to his/her last month.
